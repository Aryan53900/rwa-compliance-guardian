RWA Compliance Guardian

AI-powered compliance verification platform for Real World Asset (RWA) tokenization, combining Google Gemini AI with the Casper Blockchain to deliver transparent, automated, and immutable compliance attestations.

📌 Overview

RWA Compliance Guardian is a full-stack compliance platform designed to simplify the verification process for tokenized Real World Assets (RWAs).
The platform automates compliance screening using a custom risk engine and AI-generated assessments, then records verified attestations on the Casper Blockchain to provide an immutable audit trail.
Instead of relying on manual compliance workflows, institutions can perform automated AML-inspired screening, risk evaluation, jurisdiction checks, and blockchain-backed verification within a single platform.

🚨 Problem Statement
The rapid growth of Real World Asset tokenization introduces significant compliance challenges for financial institutions and digital asset platforms.
Traditional compliance processes are often:

Manual and time-consuming
Difficult to audit
Expensive to operate
Prone to inconsistent risk evaluation
Lacking transparent verification records

Without reliable compliance automation, onboarding investors and issuing tokenized assets becomes slower and less trustworthy.

💡 Our Solution
RWA Compliance Guardian combines:

🤖 Google Gemini AI for intelligent compliance analysis
⚖️ Custom rule-based compliance engine
⛓ Casper Blockchain smart contracts for immutable attestations
📄 Downloadable compliance reports
📊 Interactive monitoring dashboard

The result is an end-to-end compliance workflow that is transparent, scalable, and suitable for modern tokenized asset ecosystems.

🚀 Core Workflow
Investor submits compliance request.
Risk Engine evaluates AML and sanctions rules.
Gemini AI generates a compliance explanation.
Compliance decision is produced (PASS / REVIEW / FAIL).
Smart contract stores the attestation on Casper.
Dashboard, Reports, and Blockchain Explorer display the result.
PDF compliance report can be downloaded.
